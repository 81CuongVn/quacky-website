Vote for Quacky

Voting for Quacky gives you access to the following perks:

  • All Game Commands
  • Purging over 150 Messages
  • Vote Badge on your -user Profile and in -badges
  • All Voice Commands with over 10 Members Involved

You can vote for Quacky on the following websites:

  • Discord Bot Labs
  • top.gg
  • discordbotlist.com
  • Discord Boats
