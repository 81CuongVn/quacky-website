Quacky Support Server Moderation

Server Rules

Breaking one of these rules will result in a Warning:

  1. No Cursing or Racist Phrases/Slurs.
  2. No NSFW or 18+ Content.
  3. Do not spam, mass mention, or ghost ping.
  4. Use commands in their proper channel.

Reporting a User

To report a user for breaking one of our Server Rules, please do the !new command and select the "Report" category.

Warning Policy

When you hit a certain amount of warnings, you will have additional Moderation Actions taken on you.

Moderation Actions with NSFW Warnings:

  • 2 NSFW Warns: Tempban for 1 Month
  • 3 NSFW Warnings: Permanent Ban

Moderation Actions with Overall Warnings:

  • 2 Warnings: Mute for 3 Days
  • 4 Warnings: Mute for 4 Days
  • 6 Warnings: Tempban for 1 Week
  • 7 Warnings: Permanent Ban
