Moderator Information

Your Job

As a Moderator, your job is to moderate the chat and help out Support Team users in Support Tickets.

Moderators also manage partner applications and manage the suggestion queue. You have a quota of managing at least 1/3 of suggestions.

Permissions / Ranks

  • Ban Members, Logs, and Voice Moderation Permissions
  • Permissions to Partner and Suggestion Commands
  • Access to Partner Chat
