Quacky Bot Support Tickets

Being Professional in Support Tickets

Being a Support Team Member means that you are representing Quacky Bot, so please be professional in Support Tickets.

Not being professional in Support Tickets can get you removed from the Support Team!

Reports

If a user opens a ticket as a report, all online moderators (not on mobile) will be mentioned to investigate the report.

If the report is fake, just close the ticket for "Not a real report" or something else along those lines.

Ticket Blacklisting

If you believe that someone is creating fake support tickets to be a troll, please contact an admin.

Admins will investigate the situation and take moderation actions if necessary.

Support Ticket Commands

Support Team Commands

Closing Tickets

To close a ticket, do !close [reason]

The ticket user will be dm'd that the ticket is closed and, a message will say who closed it and why.

When a ticket gets closed, it gets moved to the Ticket Archive. This archive is visible to the Ticket Owner and the Rest of the Support Team.

Renaming Tickets

To rename a ticket do !rename [prefix]

The ticket "prefix" is what comes before the user's name. For example "!rename hello" would rename the ticket to #hello-username

Moderator Commands

Adding people to Tickets

To add someone to a support ticket do !add [user]

The user will be dm'd that they've been added to the ticket, and a message will say that they've been added to the ticket.

Removing people from Tickets

To remove a person from a support ticket do !remove [user]

A message will say that they've been removed from the ticket, but the user being removed will not be notified about being removed.

Transferring Ticket Owners

To transfer the ticket owner to another user do !transfer [user]

Both users (the old and new owner) will be dm'd about what happened and the ticket name will change and become #ticket-username

The old owner will have access to the ticket as if they were added to it.

NOTE: Transferring tickets to blacklisted user requires an admin.
