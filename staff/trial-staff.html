Trial Staff Information

Your Job

As a Trial Staff Member, your job is to show your Quacky Knowledge and that you can be a good Staff Member.

You also learn the Quacky Staff Policies listed below.

You're required to make at least 5 server/bot suggestions, 30 days after you’ve been hired.

You can check your suggestion amount with the !quota command.
